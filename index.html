01 · Bedtime

Your best bedtime,
every night.

Not a fixed time. TuckIn calculates tonight's bedtime from your wake-up goal, today's strain, and your sleep debt — then shifts it automatically so you're always working toward full recovery.

02 · Schedule

Know when it's
time to TuckIn.

Slide the arc to pick your wake-up time. TuckIn works backwards — calculating your exact bedtime based on your sleep goal, past history, and today's activity. Every night is different.

03 · Recovery

On-device AI that
reads your sleep.

Hard day or easy one — TuckIn reads your Apple Watch data and tells you exactly how well you recovered. Hours, efficiency, a sleep insight, and a straight answer.

04 · Performance

Two weeks of context.
One number.

Two weeks of sleep debt tracked in one chart. Spot the pattern, catch the debt before it compounds, and watch your numbers improve as the habit builds.

05 · Factors

Caffeine is just
the start.

TuckIn tracks what affects your sleep — starting with caffeine. Log your coffee and see exactly how much is still in your system at bedtime. More factors coming soon.

06 · Reminder

On your phone.
On your wrist.

TuckIn nudges you at the right time tonight — not a fixed 10pm alarm. The reminder hits your lock screen and your Apple Watch, so you actually make it to bed when it matters.
